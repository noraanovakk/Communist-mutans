Communist mutants from Space

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Communist Mutans from Space is a fixed shooter video game created in 1982 by Starpath (formerly known as Arcadia) for the Atari 2006 home video game console and the Starpath Supercharger cassette accessory.[1]
